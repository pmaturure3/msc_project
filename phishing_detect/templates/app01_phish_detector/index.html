Data & Model Accuracy

The underlying model has been trained on publicly available datasets, including the Mendeley Phishing Dataset . While these datasets provide a useful foundation for training phishing detection models, they may not fully reflect the most recent phishing techniques, patterns, or evolving threat vectors used by attackers. As a result, false positives and false negatives may occur.

Users are strongly encouraged to periodically update the training dataset and retrain the model to improve detection performance and ensure it adapts to emerging phishing strategies.
